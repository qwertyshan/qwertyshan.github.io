About Me

I love baking. I have been baking cookies in small batches since 1995. My bakery is right next to my house, in a former dairy barn.

Order

You can order my cookies by email or phone.

I can ship anywhere in the United States.
Shipping fees vary by location (and I can let you know once you order).

Since I run a small bakery, please allow one week to fulfill your order.

Feel free to ask any questions you may have about my cookies. If there are any urgent orders or special requirements, please call me and I will be glad to work with you.
