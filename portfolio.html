Product Management

I love taking products (and leading product teams) from initial concept to final launch: thinking about the big picture while delivering on the details.

Product management exists at the intersection of business, technology, and UX. My product management philosophy is deeply influenced by design thinking, lean startup, and agile principles.

I believe that great products emerge from developing true empathy for your customers, understanding their problems with clarity, and using iterative development to create simple solutions.
