Hungry Baby
iOS App

Create easy, nutritious, and varied meal plans for your baby every single week.

  • Beautiful design
  • Powerful engineering with cloud updates
  • Delicious recipes

Designed on Keynote. Built with Swift 2 on Xcode 7.

Recipe List

View all recipes as a list. Search for individual recipes. Tap on a recipe to transition to read details.

  • All recipes stored on Google Firebase backend
  • Automatic updates of recipes with every app login
  • Powerful data persistence with Core Data framework
  • Optimized image caching to reduce bandwidth utilization

Generate
Meal Plans

Automatically generate a weekly meal plan
with meals listed by day of the week.

Use a shake gesture (or tap a button)
to regenerate the meal plan.
